Brand Identity

Sage Yoga

Branding · Digital · Environmental · 2021–2023

Sage Yoga is a holistic brand developed from the ground up for a Stellenbosch-based yoga studio. Created during my final year of university, the project evolved into a living business - spanning identity, space, digital presence, and community touchpoints.

The visual language was designed to feel grounded, soft, and contemporary - balancing calm neutrality with warmth and approachability. The owner (my mum) liked lavender and sage together, the most calming colours.

Sage Yoga represents a full-scope brand built with care, continuity, and long-term intention - shaped through real use, real space, and a genuine community.

The studio ran from 2021 - 2023, and has closed since.
