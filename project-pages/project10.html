JOHNNY CLEGG

University Project · Interactive Timeline • 2022

This interactive timeline was created in honour of Johnny Clegg - not only as a musician, but as a cultural bridge during one of South Africa’s most divided times.

I deeply connected with this project. His artistry, his activism, and his courage shaped more than music - his artistry was recognised internationally, and helped reshape our culture and the way it was seen at the time.

The site explores his life chronologically, highlighting moments where music, politics and identity intersected.
