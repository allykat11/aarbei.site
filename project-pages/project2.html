Bethlehem Bouldering

UI / UX Design

Bethlehem Bouldering is a community-focused climbing guide. The project explored a digital experience that feels energetic, intuitive, and welcoming to experienced climbers looking to climb a boulder on a friend's farm.

I started with designing some logos

The owner wanted the design to be simple and fast-loading. Many guide sites struggle with performance and mobile responsiveness, so the challenge was to create a guide that works just as well on a phone as it does on a laptop. It is currently in the process of being coded into a site.
