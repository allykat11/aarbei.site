Botanical Inkdrops

Art Workshop Kit Design · Packaging · Label Design · 2021

Botanical Inkdrops was a fully assembled art workshop kit designed and produced during the Covid-19 lockdown. Each participant received a carefully packed box containing handmade botanical inks, labelled glass vials, custom boxes, and fabric-covered notebooks.

The inks were created from botanical plants and wine, resulting in organic tones and subtle variations in colour. I designed and applied every label, assembled each box, wrapped the materials, and prepared the final shipping package that was sent to participants attending the workshop online.

The project combined tactile craftsmanship with careful visual identity, ensuring the experience felt intimate and considered, even at a distance.
